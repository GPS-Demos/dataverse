A Dataverse of possibilities.

The Google Public Sector Dataverse project is designed to help public servants make better decisions for their constituents. By providing access to a wide range of data, the project can help public servants identify trends, solve problems, and improve services.

Get Started

Demographics

See trends amongst your constituents. Consider mutliple viewpoints to improve inclusion and trust.

Environment

Our habitat is essential to maintain and key to a flourishing society. Gain insights into water and air quality, fire risk, and ecosystems.

Education

Track graduation rates, teacher quality, and other important indicators. Identify areas where schools are struggling to develop programs and interventions to improve student outcomes.

Health

Understand health trends on a detailed level to understand how best to drive positive change.
